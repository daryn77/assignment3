Project: Online Caregivers Platform
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW
--------
This project implements a database system for an online caregivers platform that 
connects caregivers (babysitters, elderly care providers, playmates) with families 
seeking caregiving services. The system supports user registration, job postings, 
job applications, and appointment scheduling.


PART 1: DATABASE DESIGN (Completed)
------------------------------------
Successfully created and populated the physical database in PostgreSQL with all 
7 required tables:
  - USERS: Stores user account information (20 records)
  - CAREGIVER: Caregiver profiles with hourly rates (10 records)
  - MEMBER: Family member profiles seeking caregivers (10 records)
  - ADDRESS: Member address information (10 records)
  - JOB: Job postings by members (10 records)
  - JOB_APPLICATION: Applications from caregivers to jobs (10 records)
  - APPOINTMENT: Scheduled appointments between caregivers and members (10 records)

All primary keys, foreign keys, and relationships were properly defined.
